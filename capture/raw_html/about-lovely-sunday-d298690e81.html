The girl behind Lovely Sunday

Hi, I'm Aileen.

My love for fashion started when I was in fourth grade with two significant moments: the first time I saw Cher’s closet in Clueless (obviously!) and when my Grandpa handed me the New York Times Fashion Magazine and I fell in love with the beautiful art and clothing I discovered inside. 

I always wanted to work in fashion, but I wasn't sure of exactly what I wanted to do. I decided to start a fashion blog since I love to play dress up, have way too many shoes, and I could help people understand why fashion is so important in expressing ourselves. 

Since my husband and I are photographers- the quality, the viewpoint, and styling behind each photo is so important to me. I could not ask for someone better at understanding my vision while enjoying what we love. I hope you enjoy my passion project, learn to shop smarter, and that I can inspire you in any way.
